FAQs: Frequently asked questions

Here you will find helpful answers to the most frequently asked questions from our patients

What documents should I bring with me?

Please remember your insurance card and referral receipt from your family doctor and, if available, your old results.

Is the appointment necessary or can I come spontaneously?

Yes, an appointment must be set for treatment.

Is this practice barrier-free?

Yes.

Are there available parking spaces?

Yes, our clinic has five private parking spaces. There is other parking space in the surrounding streets.

How long should I stay in the clinic after an outpatient procedure?

This depends on the treatment being performed. On the day of the operation as well as the next day, a companion is urgently required to be able to contact the clinic in case of complications that may arise after the operation.
